# Open Questions

## Purpose
Track unresolved questions that need collective analysis.

## Question Format
- Question
- Why it matters
- Current assumptions
- Evidence needed
- Suggested owner

## Priority A: Must Address In Next 30 Days

### 1) Contribution Accounting Model
- Question: What exact unit model converts contributions into ownership claims?
- Why it matters: Core fairness and trust mechanism.
- Current assumptions: Time-based baseline with bounded multiplier.
- Evidence needed: Simulations with realistic contributor profiles.
- Suggested owner: Governance + economics lead.

### 2) Multiplier Policy
- Question: What cap and criteria should govern multipliers for scarce skills?
- Why it matters: Directly affects perceived fairness and participation.
- Current assumptions: Cap below 10x.
- Evidence needed: Stress tests on representative compensation scenarios.
- Suggested owner: Governance lead.

### 3) Initial Legal Footprint
- Question: Which jurisdiction should host the first operating entity?
- Why it matters: Determines compliance burden and speed to launch.
- Current assumptions: Start with one jurisdiction before expansion.
- Evidence needed: Comparative memo on 2-3 candidate jurisdictions.
- Suggested owner: Legal lead.

### 4) First Revenue Offer
- Question: Which digital-first service can generate earliest reliable revenue?
- Why it matters: Validates economic viability and member trust.
- Current assumptions: Begin with low-capex, skills-based services.
- Evidence needed: Customer discovery and pricing tests.
- Suggested owner: Operations lead.

### 5) Governance for v0
- Question: What minimum governance process is enough for pilot legitimacy?
- Why it matters: Prevents ad hoc decisions and conflict.
- Current assumptions: Transparent proposals, review window, explicit decisions.
- Evidence needed: Pilot governance draft and dry-run.
- Suggested owner: Governance lead.

### 6) Membership Onboarding
- Question: What entry criteria and expectations apply to pilot members?
- Why it matters: Sets culture and execution reliability.
- Current assumptions: Open entry with explicit contribution expectations.
- Evidence needed: Onboarding flow trial with first cohort.
- Suggested owner: Community lead.

## Priority B: Important, Not Blocking First 30 Days

### 7) Global Expansion Sequence
- Question: In what order should jurisdictions be added after pilot success?

### 8) Physical Goods Strategy
- Question: When should the model move from digital-first to physical goods companies?

### 9) Cross-Community Interoperability
- Question: How should different economic communities interoperate and compete fairly?

### 10) Token vs Non-Token Infrastructure
- Question: What role, if any, should blockchain tokens play in the first two years?

### 11) External Capital Policy
- Question: What funding sources are acceptable without compromising shared ownership goals?

### 12) Social Safety Mechanisms
- Question: What minimum protections should exist for contributors during low-revenue periods?

### 13) Dispute Resolution Model
- Question: What escalation path resolves contribution or governance disputes fairly?

### 14) Data Governance
- Question: How should member data rights and transparency obligations be balanced?

### 15) Long-Term Peace Impact
- Question: What measurable indicators can test whether this model reduces conflict drivers?

## Parking Rule
If a question does not block progress in the next 30 days, keep it in Priority B until it becomes operationally relevant.
