# Global Pact Ideas

## Purpose
Explore what a broadly shared cooperation pact could include.

## Possible Commitment Areas
- Peaceful conflict management
- Shared climate and resource stewardship
- Transparent cross-border cooperation norms

## Risks and Constraints
- Uneven incentives among participants
- Enforcement complexity
- Cultural and political legitimacy concerns
