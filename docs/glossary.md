# Glossary

Canonical terms for consistent writing across the repository.

## Cooperation
Working together toward shared outcomes with mutual accountability.

## Governance
Processes, roles, and decision mechanisms that guide collective action.

## Global Commons
Shared resources, knowledge, and systems stewarded for broad public benefit.

## Pact
A structured, explicit commitment among parties around principles or actions.

## Principle
A foundational rule or value used to evaluate decisions.

## Framework
An organized model that connects concepts, processes, and constraints.

## Decision Record
A document describing context, options, decision, and consequences.

## Open Question
A clearly stated unresolved issue requiring further discussion or evidence.
